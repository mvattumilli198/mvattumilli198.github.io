Horizon Retention & Success Hub

A Data-Driven Early-Alert System for Student Success

Executive Summary

The Horizon Retention & Success Hub is a unified analytics and prediction platform that helps academic advisors identify students who may be at academic risk. Using engagement, academic, attendance, and financial indicators, the platform predicts student risk tiers (Low, Medium, High) using a multi-class XGBoost model with approximately 92% accuracy. The system includes a React frontend, a Spring Boot backend with JWT authentication, and a PostgreSQL data layer.

Problem Context & Stakeholders

Academic Advisors

Need early alerts based on GPA, attendance, and engagement to prioritize outreach.

Students

Have fragmented information across LMS, grades, and financial systems.

Institution Leadership

Require aggregated insights about student performance and success patterns.

Proposed Solution

A three-tier platform integrating machine learning predictions, centralized academic data, and role-based dashboards for advisors and students.
