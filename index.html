Horizon Retention & Success Hub

A Data-Driven Early Alert Platform for Student Success

Developed by Group 5 • Horizon University

Executive Summary

The Horizon Retention & Success Hub is a student-success analytics platform designed to help advisors identify at-risk students early using academic, engagement, and financial indicators. The system integrates 11 synthetic datasets, a trained XGBoost prediction model (~92% accuracy), a secure Spring Boot backend, and a modern React dashboard for advisors and students.

Problem Context & Stakeholders

Advisors

Need a consolidated view of academic and engagement trends to spot early warning signs.

Students

Currently access fragmented information across LMS, grades, and financial portals.

University Leadership

Require visibility into retention patterns, risk groups, and intervention effectiveness.

Proposed Solution

A unified platform combining dashboards, ML-based risk scoring, academic insights, and secure advisor workflows.
