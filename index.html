Horizon Retention & Success Hub

A Data-Driven Early-Alert Platform for Student Success

Group 5 • Horizons University

Executive Summary

The Horizon Retention & Success Hub is an early-alert platform designed to help academic advisors identify and support at-risk students. It brings together GPA trends, attendance, LMS engagement, enrollments, and financial indicators for 5,000 synthetic students across four terms. A trained XGBoost model predicts Low, Medium, and High academic risk with ~92% accuracy. A React dashboard allows advisors and students to view clear insights, monitor progress, and take timely action.

Problem Context & Stakeholders

  • Advisors struggle to track risk signals spread across multiple systems.
  • Students rely on fragmented views of grades, attendance, and financial status.
  • Universities require better visibility into retention patterns.

Proposed Solution

Our platform provides unified dashboards, ML-powered risk predictions, and advisor workflows for intervention.
